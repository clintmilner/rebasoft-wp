Forensics

Forensics are accessible from devices and interfaces, and allow users to access the raw data collected. Forensics allows a series of reports to be run that can isolate activity down to a single url accessed at a point in time where the data is stored. See Appendix 2Forensics reports by data type for a list of available report by data collection type:

note:
Forensics views are accessed via the inspect icon

When in forensics, reports can be selected and filters applied. Reports are available as graphs, or access to raw data can be achieved by selecting tabular data.

Filters can be applied by selecting the field from the drop down, and entering the required data:

When chosen filter element is entered click the '+' button to add. This will add a highlighted filter item:

Click the 'Ok' button and your view will now be filtered as required.

A second method of applying filters is to click on the chart indicator icon for your selected record and select your required element. This selection will then be populated into the filter editor. Click the ‘Ok’ button and your view will now be filtered as required:
