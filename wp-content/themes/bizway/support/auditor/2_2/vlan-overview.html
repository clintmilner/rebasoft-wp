VLAN overview

From the device overview page, selecting a VLAN from the list at the bottom of the page will produce a VLAN overview showing details for the selected VLAN, including device information.

Note: selecting a VLAN from within an interface section of the device overview will only take you to the Interface overview.



Note:

VLAN is "local" is the default VLAN that appears in many devices even if no VLA's are configured.
