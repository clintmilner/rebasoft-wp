MAC forensics

MAC forensics allows users to review the IP and connection history of a MAC address:

Auditor shows the MAC address, and from the Vendor lookup facility, the Vendor or Manufacturer that has the IP Address assigned.

If there is an ARP entry on a device for the MAC address, Auditor will show the IP address allocation history of the MAC address. The IP details show the history of IP address assignment to the MAC address.

The Connection history shows the connections over time. This is available in graphical timeline view or tabular view if details are selected.

Connections show the interfaces (and device) that the MAC address has been seen on (The MAC Count shows the total number of unique MAC's also on the interface).
